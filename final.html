It's a full circle — nobody's unbeatable. The team that troubled us (Twin Cities) is one New York handled easily, and the team that beat New York (Heartland) is one we demolished. That's why the tape says toss-up, and why it comes down to who plays their game on the day.

Four keys to lifting the Cup

1
Survive the first quarter. New York try to bury teams early — both their semi goals came inside 8 minutes. Stay level early and the game opens up for us.
2
Go for goals, not just points. Goals are the only thing that has beaten them. Back ourselves to take the green-flag chances.
3
Share the scoring. Our goals came from all over the pitch last day — they can't key on one player.
4
Finish stronger. We score in the second half. Keep the intensity to the final whistle — that's where we win it.
